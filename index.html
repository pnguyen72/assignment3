Bonnie

Bonnie is an animatronic rabbit and the guitarist in Freddy's band, positioned at the left side of the stage. He has a personality based on a rock musician; boastful, prideful, with the speech pattern of a laid back hippie stereotype.

Freddy

Freddy is a brown animatronic bear and lead singer of the band, standing in the center of the stage. He enjoys having fun and being laid-back, easygoing, happy-go-lucky, despite coming off as a threatening and scary big brown bear.

Chica

Chica is an animatronic chicken and the backup singer in Freddy's band, positioned at the right side of the stage. She is a pushy, gluttonous but lovable singing animatronic bot who has love for food, especially pizza.

Foxy

Foxy is a discontinued animatronic pirate fox entertainer. He resides at his own separate stage in the pizzeria. His personality is that of a stereotypical pirate, often speaking in pirate slang, all with a pirate accent.
